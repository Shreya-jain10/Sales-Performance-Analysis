#  Global Bicycle Sales Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the sales performance of **Global Bicycle Sales Analysis Dashboard**, a global bicycle manufacturing company. The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights using **Microsoft Excel**, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions.

The project involves:
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- KPI creation using Excel formulas
- Pivot table analysis
- Interactive dashboard development
- Business insight generation

---

##  Business Objective
To analyze historical sales data and answer key business questions related to:

- Revenue and profit performance
- Product profitability
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Country-wise performance
- Sales trends and growth patterns

##  Data Cleaning & Preparation

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

- Handled missing values in **Profit** and **Cost** columns.
- Standardized customer gender values (e.g., `F`, `FeMale` → `Female`).
- Removed inconsistencies and duplicate records.
- Created calculated columns for:
  - Profit
  - Revenue
  - Profit Margin %
- Built KPIs using:
  - `SUMIF`
  - `COUNTIF`
  - `AVERAGE`
  - `IF`
  - Lookup and aggregation functions

---

##  Key Business Insights

###  Highest Profit Generating Country
- **United States** generated the highest overall profit.

###  Most Profitable Product Category
- **Road Bikes** emerged as the most profitable category.

###  Order Quantity vs Profitability
- Higher order quantities do **not always** result in higher profits.
- Several bulk orders generated comparatively lower profit margins.

###  Highest Sales Growth
- **2015** recorded the highest year-over-year sales growth.

###  High Revenue but Lower Profitability
- **Road Bikes** generated significant revenue but showed lower profitability in certain segments due to higher costs and discounts.

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Focus on expanding operations in the **United States**, the most profitable market.
- Optimize pricing and discount strategies for **Road Bikes**.
- Investigate bulk orders with low margins to improve profitability.
- Increase marketing efforts in high-growth regions and products.
- Monitor product costs to improve profit margins.
